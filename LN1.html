ASME TKMCE

1. ORIENTATION PROGRAMME FOR FRESHMEN

The ASME TKMCE chapter organized a freshmen orientation program on the 18th of March 2019 in which around 130 fresher’s attended. The orientation was led by Mr. Sriram Sankar, former Chairman of the ASME student chapter of FISAT and nominee for the ASME Charles T Main Student leadership award. Dr. Rijo Jacob Thomas, the Faculty Coordinator presided over the function. The session was a briefing about the history of ASME, the student scholarship programmes, engineering festivals such as EFest, EFx, and other benefits of ASME membership such as – free subscription to the ASME monthly magazine, online libraries and other internship programmes. It was an interactive session where the freshman got an idea about what is to be done in order to shape their future in the industry, while using ASME as a stepping stone.

2. FRESHMEN RECRUITMENT

A recruitment test was conducted by the student chapter for the freshmen which consisted of 2 rounds- a screening test and an idea pitching round.

A total of 120 freshmen from the mechanical and mechanical production branches of the college appeared for the initial screening test out of which, 45 were selected for the next round. In the idea pitching, the selected students were split into groups of 5 and were made to give ideas for a given problem statement – which was to envision a feasible, practical instrument which can be used in times of natural disasters as an aid to the rescue activities

The idea pitching round ended up receiving incredible response, with the freshmen giving wonderful ideas and after the round, 15 were selected for final recruitment.

3. STUDENT DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM (SDP)

The Student Development Program is an initiative taken by ASME TKMCE chapter to improve the Technical and soft skills of fellow members. It is an after class session were fellow senior members share their skills on to junior members to cultivate leadership, basic technical skills and soft skills. As the first step a two day class on basics of Design and Introduction of Fusion 360 was conducted, in which more than 40 members other skills such as programming, communication etc. are planned.

4. IDEA PITCHING COMPETITION:

Pitch Stop Season 1 was a competitive event coordinated by the ASME TKMCE chapter. It was a two-part idea pitching competition on the topic ‘Mobility Devices’ which was open for participants from all colleges. The first part was the document submission round where the participants were required to mail in a detailed report on their innovative ideas. These reports underwent screening and the selected participants went on to participate in the second round. The second round required the participants to make a presentation on their innovation and present it over the online meeting platform. The winners were selected and prizes worth ₹1000 were awarded.

6. EFFECTO THEATRE:

Effecto Theatre was a competitive event organized by the ASME TKMCE chapter which was conducted in connection with the world science day celebration. It was conducted as an online treasure hunt competition on 14th and 15th November 2020 on the popular social networking platform Instagram. Entries were free for participants from any college. Short video snippets from movies or the internet portraying scientific theories or effects were posted as stories on the official Instagram handle of ASME TKMCE (@asmetkmce). Participants were required to find out what the effect or theory was and message it to the Instagram handle within the provided time. The first participants to figure out what effects each were awarded the prizes. Furthermore, the person who referred the most number of people to participate in the competition was also awarded a prize. Around 20 -25 participants from multiple colleges participated in this event.

8. BRAIN SCAN:

Brain scan was a two part competitive event conducted by the ASME TKMCE chapter exclusively for the freshers in February 2021. The first part was an aptitude quiz, conducted online. The winners from this round were made into groups of 4-5 members for the second round. The second round was an Idea pitching round on the topic Methods to adopt renewable energy. The winners were decided by former executive members Athul TP and Sayuj Jayadev. Prizes worth ₹4000 were awarded to the winners.

9. 3D PRINTING WEBINAR:

3D printing has revolutionised how we approach manufacturing. The ASME TKMCE chapter organised a webinar on the topic of 3D printing- An innovative Technology to Empower Engineers. It was conducted on 9 January 2021 on google meet. It gave the attendees an insight into the various parameters involved in 3D printing, the scope of its application and the role that it plays in industry 4.0.
